40 Resume Writing Tips which you could read in less than 2 minutes!


Resume Preparation is not a one day activity. How to write a resume that attracts recruiters and gets the interview call is what every jobseeker wants to know. Do you know this-Recruiters only give 6 seconds to you resume? Naukri.com surveyed and found out that average time a recruiter is only 5-7 seconds. With the recruiters receiving countless resumes online and offline, what you should do as a jobseeker to stand out in the crowd.

Resume Writing is easy if you follow the job resume tips given by Naukri Fast Forward writers. Make your resume flawless and customize according to the job opening you are applying.

Resume writing for freshers is always challenging as you do not have an experience section. Writing skills and achievements for the experienced professionals is difficult as you do not want to miss out anything important yet keep the resume crisp and clutter free. The formatting for resume also has to follow certain rules. With so many pointers to follow, resume preparation gives you a headache.
